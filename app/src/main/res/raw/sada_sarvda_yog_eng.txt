Sarvamukhi mangala bolavave ||

Fanindra maatha mukuti jalali |
Kaarunya sindhu bhava dukha haari |
Tujhvin shembho maja kona taari ||

Morya morya mi baal taane |
Tujhicha seva karukaaya jane |
Anyaaya majha kotyaana koti |
Moreshwaraba tu ghala poti ||

Tya tya thikani nij rupa tujhe |
